# Pig Game
 
[Live Link](https://pig-game-fun.netlify.app/)

A fun and quirky game where you roll a dice and it adds the dice amount to your current score, but be careful if you don't choose to Hold the score and add it to your total and you roll a 'Snake Eye' (1), you give up the points and hand the dice over to your friend! 

First to 100 points wins!

Built with vanilla JavaScript.
